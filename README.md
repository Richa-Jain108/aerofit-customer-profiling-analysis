# 🏃 AeroFit Customer Profiling & Probability Analysis

### Customer Segmentation, Descriptive Statistics & Probability Analysis using Python

This project analyzes customer purchasing behavior across AeroFit's treadmill product portfolio using **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**, **Descriptive Statistics**, and **Probability Analysis**.

The objective is to identify customer characteristics associated with each treadmill model, uncover purchasing patterns, and generate actionable recommendations that can help AeroFit improve product positioning, customer targeting, and sales performance.

## 🎯 Business Problem

AeroFit offers three treadmill models targeting different customer segments:

| Product | Segment                | Price  |
| ------- | ---------------------- | ------ |
| KP281   | Entry-Level            | $1,500 |
| KP481   | Mid-Level Runner       | $1,750 |
| KP781   | Premium Advanced Model | $2,500 |

The AeroFit marketing team wants to better understand:

* Which customer segments purchase each treadmill model
* How demographics influence product selection
* What factors differentiate premium customers from standard customers
* How probability can be used to predict customer purchase behavior
* How customer insights can improve product recommendations and marketing effectiveness

The ultimate goal is to develop customer profiles that support data-driven decision-making and improve customer-product fit.

---

## 📊 Dataset Overview

The dataset contains customer information collected from individuals who purchased AeroFit treadmills during the previous three months.

### Dataset Features

| Feature       | Description                               |
| ------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Product       | Treadmill purchased (KP281, KP481, KP781) |
| Age           | Customer age                              |
| Gender        | Male / Female                             |
| Education     | Years of education                        |
| MaritalStatus | Single / Partnered                        |
| Usage         | Planned treadmill usage per week          |
| Income        | Annual income                             |
| Fitness       | Self-rated fitness score (1–5)            |
| Miles         | Expected weekly walking/running distance  |

## 📈 Key Insights

### Customer Demographics

* Most customers fall within the young to middle-aged adult segment.
* Male customers represent a larger share of treadmill purchases.
* Partnered customers demonstrate stronger purchasing activity than single customers.

### Product Purchasing Patterns

* KP281 is the most frequently purchased treadmill.
* KP481 serves as the primary mid-tier option.
* KP781 attracts a specialized premium customer segment.

### Premium Customer Characteristics

Customers purchasing KP781 generally exhibit:

* Higher annual income
* Higher fitness ratings
* Greater expected weekly mileage
* More frequent treadmill usage

### Customer Behavior

* Fitness level, usage frequency, and income are strong indicators of product selection.
* Premium treadmill purchases are strongly associated with higher customer engagement and fitness commitment.
